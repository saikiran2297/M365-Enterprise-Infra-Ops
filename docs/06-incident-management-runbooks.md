# Incident Management (3rd Line)

## Objectives
- Reduce time-to-triage and time-to-recovery
- Provide consistent comms and leadership during SEV events
- Capture learning and preventative actions (PIR)

## Runbook standards
Every runbook contains:
- Scope + impact definition (SEV1/2/3)
- Immediate triage steps (first 10 minutes)
- Containment & mitigation
- Recovery steps
- Validation checklist
- Escalation path & vendor engagement
- PIR requirements

## Runbooks
- Auth outage: `runbooks/incidents/sev1-auth-outage.md`
- Network outage: `runbooks/incidents/sev1-network-outage.md` (add later)
- Sync failure: `runbooks/incidents/entra-connect-sync-failure.md`
